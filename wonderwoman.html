WONDER WOMAN

BIO


Princess Diana is the daughter of Queen Hippolyta, Queen of the Amazons, who molded her out of clay and asked the Greek Pantheon of Gods to bring her to life

As Wonder Woman, she has super-strength, speed, and agility. A founding member of the Justice League and the Trinity together with Batman and Superman they are Earth's greatest superheroes. Trained in the art of war as an amazon she is described as the world's greatest melee fighter by Batman and could go toe to toe with Superman as she has on many occasions and won. She flies an invisible plane and can fly (at first by riding wind currents) but later being gifted flight oi up to supersonic speeds by the Greek God Hermes. Her bracelets are indestructible and her magic lasso compels people to tell the truth.
